# Remindly — Voice Reminder Widget

The fastest way to capture what you can't forget. Tap the mic on the widget or app icon, say it naturally, done.

## Voice widget — always one tap away

Add the Remindly widget to your home screen and create reminders without unlocking your phone. Speak in plain language:
• "Remind me in 5 minutes to lock the door"
• "Tomorrow at 9 call the dentist"
• "Every day at 8:30 take vitamins"
• "In 3 months revisit the dentist"

Works in Ukrainian, English, Spanish and German.

## Google Calendar — sync and export

Export any reminder to Google Calendar with one tap directly from the notification or event list. Enable auto-export so every new reminder lands in your Google Calendar automatically.

Start any voice command with "Google" to create the event straight in Google Calendar, skipping Remindly's list entirely:
• "Google every day at 9:30 take vitamins" → daily recurring event
• "Google in 5 months visit the dentist" → single event

## Location-triggered reminders

Save your places (home, office, gym, shop) and bind any reminder to a location. The notification fires automatically when you arrive — no time needed.

## Smart reminders

- **Timed reminders** — one-off or recurring, with snooze buttons you configure yourself
- **No-time reminders** — pinned to status bar, triggered when you arrive at a saved location
- **Status bar countdown** — always see how long until the next reminder
- **Pinning & repeat** — pin any notification permanently; tap 🔁 to repeat every minute

## Share reminders

Send a reminder to anyone as a Google Calendar link. They tap it — it saves to their calendar.

## Backup & restore

Save all reminders, tasks, and locations to a file. Restore with one tap.
